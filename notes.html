Description Tags

<strong>: Strong
Text wrapped in strong tags will be showed as bolded text.

<mark>: Mark
Wrap the text that you want to highlighted.

<sub>: Subscript
Text will appear smaller and dropped down half a line, usually used for chemical formulas (e.g.: H2O).

<sup>: Superscript
Text can be used for mathematical equations (e.g.: x3).

<abbr>: Abbreviations
Use abreviation tag form for a word (e.g.: The WHO was founded in 1948).
